A week of Camp- A Chance at Life

Camp Rhino is a new Christian camp in Port Elizabeth, South Africa that plans to officially open in July 2018. Our mission is to change lives. We want to change the lives of South African children through a week of camp where they will learn the truths of the Bible and change the lives of missions minded American students by giving them the opportunity to be camp counselors in Africa.

Campers will experience a week of exciting games and activities in a fun safe environment. Most of all, they will learn the life changing message of the Bible from people who care.

Counselors from all over the world will get to spend a season in South Africa ministering the Gospel in at camp. They will also get to take part in and help in a South African church and will no doubt make memories that will last a lifetime.
